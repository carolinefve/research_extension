How to Use Research Insights

  1. Visit a supported research site

    Navigate to arXiv, PubMed, IEEE Xplore, Google Scholar, Springer, or ACM Digital Library

  2. Open a research paper

    The extension will automatically detect the paper and show a green indicator

  3. Click "Analyze Current Page"

    Processing takes about 30 seconds to extract insights including key findings, methodology, and research gaps

  4. Review insights and connections

    View the analysis and discover how this paper relates to previously analyzed papers

  5. Export your analysis

    Save the current paper's analysis as a PDF for future reference
