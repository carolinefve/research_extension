How to Use Research Insights

  1. Visit a supported research site

    Navigate to arXiv, PubMed, IEEE Xplore, Google Scholar, Springer, or ACM Digital Library

  2. Open a research paper

    The extension works with both HTML pages and PDF files. PDF extraction may take a bit longer but includes full paper content.

  3. Click "Analyse Current Page"

    Processing takes about 30-60 seconds to extract insights including title, abstract, introduction, key findings, methodology, conclusion, and research gaps

  4. Review insights and connections

    View the analysis and discover how this paper relates to previously analysed papers

  5. Export your analysis

    Save the current paper's analysis as a PDF for future reference
